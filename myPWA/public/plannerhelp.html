How to use Study Planner

1.Start by entering your task in the input bar and when the task is due

If you don't have a due date for your task, you can leave the due date blank

2.Once your information has been added you can click "Add Task" to create a task card

3. Once the task card shows up, your task will be saved until you delete it!

4.If you wish to delete your task then click the "Delete Task" located at the bottom of each task card
